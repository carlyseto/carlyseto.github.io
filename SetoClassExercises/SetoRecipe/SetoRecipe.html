Mac and Cheese

Homemade Mac and Cheese Recipe!

Ingredients

  • macaroni
  • cheddar cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • milk
  • butter
  • flour
  • bread crumbs
  • paprika

Directions

  1. Cook macaroni and drain
  2. Melt butter and add flour, milk, and cheeses
  3. Pour into dish bowl
  4. Add sauce and stir
  5. Melt butter in skillet then add breadcrumbs
  6. Sprinkle with paprika
  7. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes
